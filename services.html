SERVICES

Where I Can Help

Available for SMSF audit support, process consulting, and conversations about full-time opportunities — depending on what your firm needs.

WP-01

SMSF Audit & Compliance Support

Problem this solves: SMSF audits stall when workpapers are inconsistent or audit-readiness is left until the last minute.

What's included: End-to-end SMSF audit workflow leadership, regulatory accuracy review, and audit-ready documentation delivered to your timeline.

How it works: Scoping call → review of current workpaper process → engagement on agreed files or as an extension of your audit team.

WP-02

SMSF Workpaper Preparation & Review

Problem this solves: Inconsistent workpapers slow down review and create rework between preparer and reviewer.

What's included: Detailed, audit-ready workpaper preparation and review, built on standardised templates that hold up under scrutiny.

How it works: Share current templates and a sample file → I assess gaps → we agree on a preparation or review cadence.

WP-03

AI-Driven Audit Process Automation

Problem this solves: Manual, repetitive audit and compliance tasks eat into time that should go to higher-value review.

What's included: Excel and no-code AI automation built around your team's actual workflow — for financial reporting, compliance documentation, and recurring checks.

How it works: Audit your current process → identify the highest-friction manual steps → build and test automation with human review kept in the loop.

WP-04

Finance BPO Operations Advisory

Problem this solves: Scaling a finance BPO team without losing quality, consistency, or client trust.

What's included: Operational guidance on team supervision, knowledge transfer, and quality consistency across associates and client files.

How it works: Conversation about current team structure and pain points → recommendations or hands-on support, scoped to what's actually needed.

Engagement & Pricing

Scoped to the Work, Not a Fixed Package

Every firm's audit process is a little different, so engagement length and pricing are discussed after an initial scoping conversation rather than fixed in advance. I'm also open to full-time and career conversations — just say so when you reach out.
