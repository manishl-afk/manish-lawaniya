CASE STUDIES

Process Improvements, Not Just Job Titles

Three real changes I made to how SMSF audit work gets done — what was slow, what I changed, and what happened next.

CS-01 · Senior Associate, Befree Global

Workpaper Turnaround Optimisation

Rebuilding Excel-based audit templates to cut review cycle time on SMSF engagements.

Challenge

Manual, ad-hoc Excel templates for workpaper preparation created inconsistent structure between files, slowing down review and creating unnecessary back-and-forth between preparer and reviewer.

Approach

Rebuilt the core Excel templates used across SMSF audit workpapers — standardising formulas, layout, and cross-references so reviewers could find what they needed without asking.

Result

Reduced average review cycle time and freed up audit capacity for higher-value review work instead of formatting and reconciliation chasing.

Microsoft Excel Audit Workpapers Template Standardisation

Lesson learned: small standardisation changes compound — a clean template saves more time at review than it costs at setup.

CS-02 · Associate, Befree Global

Compliance Workflow Automation

Building reusable Excel tools to standardise recurring compliance tasks across SMSF client files.

Challenge

Recurring compliance checks across multiple SMSF client files were handled manually and inconsistently depending on which associate was assigned to the file.

Approach

Built reusable Excel-based tools to standardise and partially automate the most repetitive compliance checks, designed around how the work actually got done day to day.

Result

More consistent compliance output across associates and client files, with less time spent re-explaining process steps to new team members.

Excel Automation Fund Accounting Process Standardisation

Lesson learned: automation gets adopted when it's built by the person doing the work — it matches the real workflow instead of an idealised one.

CS-03 · Ongoing, Deputy Manager, Befree Global

AI Integration Into SMSF Audit Workflows

Introducing generative AI and no-code automation into financial reporting and compliance documentation — with human review kept firmly in the loop.

Challenge

Even after process standardisation, manual drafting of financial reporting and compliance documentation still consumed significant audit time.

Approach

Following certification as an AI-Driven Professional, began introducing generative AI and no-code automation tools at specific drafting stages — while keeping human review as the final compliance gate, not a formality.

Result

Early-stage rollout focused on cutting manual drafting time without reducing audit oversight. This is an active, ongoing initiative rather than a finished project.

Generative AI No-Code Automation Power BI

Lesson learned: AI earns its place in audit work by removing drafting time — never by removing judgement.
