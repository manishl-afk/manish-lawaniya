Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you visit manishl-afk.github.io/manish-lawaniya (the "Site"), the personal professional website of Manishkumar Lawaniya ("I", "me").

1. Information I Collect

This Site does not run its own server-side backend or database. The Contact page includes a form (Name, Email, Company, Phone, Purpose of Contact, Message). When you submit it, your browser opens your own email client with those details pre-filled in a message addressed to me — the information is sent directly from your device to my inbox via your email provider. It is not collected, stored, or processed by this website or by any third party on my behalf.

If you email me directly instead of using the form, that exchange is governed by your own email provider's privacy practices and mine.

2. Cookies & Analytics

This Site does not currently use cookies, analytics, or visitor-tracking tools. If that changes in future (for example, if I add Google Analytics or a scheduling tool), this policy will be updated to reflect it before those tools go live.

This Site loads fonts from Google Fonts. Visiting any page may cause your browser to request font files from Google's servers, which can transmit your IP address to Google in line with Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Third-Party Links

This Site links to external platforms — including LinkedIn and Befree Global — that I don't control. Their own privacy policies govern any information you share with them.

4. Data Security

Because this Site does not store visitor or form data on a server, there is no database of visitor information to secure or that could be exposed in a breach. Any information you choose to email me is protected according to standard email provider security practices.

5. Your Choices

If you've previously sent me information via the contact form or by email and would like me to delete it from my inbox or records, just email me and I'll action that request.

6. Children's Privacy

This Site is a professional portfolio and is not directed at, or intended for use by, children.

7. Changes to This Policy

I may update this policy as the Site evolves — for example, if I add analytics, a booking tool, or a managed contact form. The "Last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent version.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to manish.l@superrecords.com.au.
