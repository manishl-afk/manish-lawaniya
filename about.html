The Person Behind the Workpapers

From articled assistant to Deputy Manager — a 13-year path through accounting, audit, and AI-driven process design.

Introduction

My Story

I'm a finance professional with over 13 years of experience across accounting, audit, and BPO operations — the last six of which have been focused entirely on SMSF audit and compliance. I currently serve as Deputy Manager at Befree Global, where I lead SMSF audit workflows, review workpapers for accuracy and compliance, supervise junior associates, and collaborate directly with Australian accounting firms to deliver audit-ready documentation on time.

My path through accounting, auditing, and finance BPO operations has given me a holistic view of both the technical and operational sides of SMSF services. I bring efficiency, accuracy, and value through process optimisation, data-driven decision-making, and clear stakeholder communication.

Most recently, I became a Certified AI-Driven Professional through Be10x's AI Career Accelerator Inner Circle Program. I'm now actively integrating generative AI and no-code automation into SMSF audit workflows, financial reporting, and compliance documentation — bridging traditional finance practice with the AI-powered future of audit work.

Why I Do This

Mission & Values

Every SMSF audit protects someone's retirement savings. Getting the detail right matters — not because a regulator is watching, but because the numbers represent someone's future.

Accuracy Without Exception

A workpaper is either right or it isn't. I review line-by-line because compliance leaves no room for "close enough."

Augment, Don't Replace

AI should remove repetitive manual work from an audit — never the judgement. I build automation that supports review, not shortcuts around it.

Communication Across Time Zones

Working with Australian clients from India means deadlines and documentation have to be clear the first time — no back-and-forth required.
